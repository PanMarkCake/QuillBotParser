## Network Automation: What Is It? The process of managing and controlling network infrastructure and devices using software and automation technologies is known as network automation. Increased network agility, consistency, and dependability result from automating setup, deployment, monitoring, and troubleshooting procedures. Automation carries out these duties in accordance with predetermined policies and workflows rather than depending on human operations. Automation platforms, configuration management tools, and script-based methods are frequently used to accomplish this. Among the advantages of network automation are:
